<|startoftext|>This is called a wildfire. I might be hearing a lot about wildfires on the news right now. "#<|endoftext|>
<|startoftext|>I also might see smoke in the air. The smoke can make it look hazy and can make the air smell bad. . $% Emergency workers, like firefighters and police officers, are working to keep me safe.<|endoftext|>
<|startoftext|>&' If a fire gets too close to our home, we will need to leave. This is called evacuation When people evacuate, they go somewhere that is further away from the fire.<|endoftext|>
<|startoftext|>If we evacuate, there is usually time to pack some of my favorite clothes and toys. () I can go back home when it is safe. My family will always keep me safe, no matter where we go.<|endoftext|>
<|startoftext|>I can also do some yoga or stretches to calm my mind and my body. If I am worried, I can talk to my family or an adult. I can also take deep breaths to help me stay calm. !!!* !!<|endoftext|>
